Q StudioLab offers three programs to serve the educational and social needs of children in our diverse community:

1. QstudioLab School

We are currently accepting Kindergarten -Third Grade Students for the current 2017-2018 school year. We are expanding by 1-2 grade each new academic year.

At our school, students exercise their head, heart and body. We recognize that children are innately curious and eager learners. All students are engaged in the learning process through project based methods and learning lab explorations. Core academic skills are imbedded in all activities. Students have the opportunity for real life applications as we expand our classroom into our community during our weekly adventure and community service days.

2. PreQ DAYCARE

Full or part time options for 2-3 year olds

Also available are MORNING INFANT/TODDLER CLASSES in which the caregiver or mom and child interact together a guide, playing in a variety of experiential mediums, such as water, sand, textures, and clay.

3. AFTERSCHOOL AT THE Q

Afterschool services and programs are available for 5-12 year olds. We will pick up students at our local area schools.

Some of our offerings are art studios: science lab, library study hall, storytelling, cooking, dance, language classes in Chinese and Spanish, academic tutoring.

We also offer intensive master classes by professional artists and educators for children who want to explore a subject or art form in depth.
